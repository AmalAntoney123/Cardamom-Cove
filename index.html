About The Cardamom Cove

A Sanctuary in Idukki

Located in Kauwanty, Idukki, The Cardamom Cove is a luxury retreat surrounded by the lush greenery of a cardamom estate. Enjoy the cool climate, stunning views of the Western Ghats, and the tranquil waters of the Idukki Reservoir.
